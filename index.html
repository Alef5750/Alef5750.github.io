How to Make a Peanut butter and Jelly Sandwitch

  1. Get a jar of peanut butter, jelly, and bread.
  2. Open the jar of peanut butter.
  3. Take a knife, and while holding the handle, insert the blade into the jar of peanut butter.
  4. Using the blade, remove peanut butter from its jar, and place it on a slice of bread.
  5. Spread the peanut butter until it has covered the entire surface of the slice.
  6. After wiping the blade clean on the second slice, do the same with the jelly.
  7. Place the spreaded sides of the slices on one another.
  8. Wash netilat yadayim
  9. Say a bracha of "hamotzi".
  10. B'te'avon!
